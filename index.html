I stumbled into product management. Felt lost. Asked stupid questions. Got lucky.

Strangers became mentors. People I'd never meet in person took time to guide me. That's the Silicon Valley way – people help because they can. Because someone once helped them.

Now it's my turn.

I'm setting aside time each week for anyone looking to break into product management. New grads. Career changers. Anyone feeling stuck.

Just reach out if you'd like to chat.

This isn't a funnel into paid coaching. It's free. Always will be. Chat once or monthly – up to you. No question is too basic.

Just people helping people. Like others did for me.
