UX Design

Providing an intuitive and engaging user experience is the most crucial aspect of creating a successful product.

I have worked across the full spectrum of user centred design for internal teams, large organisations and public institutions.

I have also lead design teams as well as trained others in using UX techniques and principles.

My design process includes:

  • Stakeholder workshops
  • Collaborative sketching
  • Lean UX
  • User research
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • User testing

I believe strongly that a great user experience is the responsibility of the whole team rather than one person. I therefore have spent a lot of my time getting everyone involved in the design and user testing process.
