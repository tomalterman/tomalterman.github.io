UX Design

Providing an intuitive and engaging user experience is the most crucial aspect of creating a successful product or service.

Whether for internal teams, large organisations or public institutions, I have worked across the full spectrum of user centred design, including:

  • Stakeholder workshops
  • User research
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • User testing

I believe however that a great user experience is the responsibility of the whole team rather than one person. I therefore spend a lot of my time getting everyone involved in the design process, providing insights from research or testing, as well as teaching the core principles of UX design.
