Product Management

Whilst working with product-focussed tech startups I have learnt the importance of a effective product management.

Having been in charge of development and design teams, I have taken up more and more product management responsiblities to ensure that those teams work effectively and that we are always tackling the most important business problem.

My experience includes:

  • Identifying the Minimum Viable Product
  • Managing Agile developement projects
  • Identifying and gathering effective business metrics and KPIs
  • Writing effective user stories
  • Developing and validating product hypotheses
  • Data analysis

I believe however that a great user experience is the responsibility of the whole team rather than one person. I therefore spend a lot of my time getting everyone involved in the design process, providing insights from research or testing, as well as teaching the core principles of UX design.
